Introduction of "RWC Music Database: Classical Music"

Overview:

The Classical Music Database consists of 50 pieces:

  • Symphonies: 4 pieces
  • Concerti: 2 pieces
  • Orchestral music: 4 pieces
  • Chamber music: 10 pieces
  • Solo performances: 24 pieces
  • Vocal performances: 6 pieces
All 50 public-domain pieces were originally performed and recorded for our database (not all movements were recorded: a certain movement was selected and recorded for several categories such as symphony and concerto).

More detailed information about this database is described in our paper in the references.
